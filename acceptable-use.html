Acceptable Use Policy

This policy describes activities and content prohibited when using FanPaid.

Last updated: July 13, 2026

FanPaid is pre-launch. This policy establishes baseline rules for the moderation, verification, and enforcement systems we are designing.

1. Purpose

FanPaid is intended for lawful creative, community, commercial, and professional activity. Users must comply with applicable law, our Terms, and platform policies.

2. Illegal or Harmful Activity

Users may not facilitate unlawful activity, fraud, scams, money laundering, sanctions evasion, trafficking, extortion, credible threats, or illegal goods or services.

3. Protection of Minors

FanPaid has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material, grooming, sexual exploitation of minors, or content that sexualizes or endangers minors. Suspected violations may result in immediate account action, preservation of relevant records, and reports where required by law.

4. Exploitation and Non-Consensual Content

Non-consensual intimate imagery, sexual exploitation, coercion, trafficking, hidden-camera sexual content, and content published without necessary consent are prohibited. Any age-restricted category FanPaid elects to support will be subject to separate eligibility, identity, age, consent, moderation, and payment requirements.

5. Harassment and Abuse

Users may not threaten, stalk, doxx, or engage in targeted harassment or promote unlawful violence or discrimination.

6. Platform Integrity

Malware, unauthorized access, credential theft, security bypass, spam, fake engagement, manipulated referrals, fraudulent reviews, payment abuse, and enforcement evasion are prohibited.

7. Intellectual Property and Privacy

Do not publish content you lack the right to use. Users must respect copyright, trademark, privacy, publicity, confidentiality, and other legal rights.

8. Enforcement

FanPaid may investigate suspected violations; remove content; restrict features; require verification; suspend or terminate accounts; preserve records; or report matters where required by law.

9. Report a Concern

Report policy or safety concerns to safety@fanpaid.com or hello@fanpaid.com.
