Pre-Launch Compliance Program

PROGRAM IN DEVELOPMENT

FanPaid is evaluating identity, payment, infrastructure, moderation, and compliance providers while developing operational controls before opening the full service.

Compliance Framework

Identity & Age

Identity checks, age assurance, liveness, and re-verification where appropriate.

Payments & Fraud

Provider controls, transaction review, audit trails, and fraud signals.

Content Governance

Policies, reports, moderation queues, appeals, and escalation.

Privacy & Security

Data minimization, permissions, logging, and incident processes.

Payment and Financial Compliance

FanPaid intends to use qualified third-party providers for relevant transaction services. Payment methods, creator categories, geographies, and payouts may depend on underwriting, provider requirements, sanctions controls, fraud risk, and applicable law.

Identity, KYC, and KYB

Creators, payout recipients, businesses, organizations, or other account types may be required to complete identity or business verification. External provider results may be combined with internal risk and manual review workflows.

Privacy

Our privacy program is intended to address transparency, purpose limitation, retention, user rights, vendor management, and international data considerations. See the Privacy Policy.

Content and Safety

See our Acceptable Use Policy, Creator Guidelines, and Trust & Safety Center.

Security and Auditability

The platform is being designed around least-privilege access, role and permission controls, audit logging, secure secret handling, monitoring, and documented administrative workflows.

Compliance Inquiries

Providers and compliance partners may contact compliance@fanpaid.com or hello@fanpaid.com.
