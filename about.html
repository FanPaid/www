Our Mission

Our mission is to give creators more control over how they publish, connect with audiences, build communities, and earn from their work.

We believe creator businesses deserve serious tools for content, monetization, analytics, communication, collaboration, and operations.

What We Are Building

FanPaid is being developed as an all-in-one creator platform supporting publishing, livestreaming, subscriptions, tips, digital products, communities, messaging, business opportunities, bookings, teams, organizations, analytics, and developer integrations.

The platform is intended for musicians, artists, educators, comedians, photographers, videographers, streamers, influencers, coaches, mechanics, developers, and other skilled or creative professionals.

Our Principles

Creator-first

Tools built around sustainable creator businesses.

Trust by design

Verification, moderation, permissions, and auditability.

Broad creativity

Support many legitimate creator categories.

Responsible growth

Develop safety and compliance alongside monetization.
