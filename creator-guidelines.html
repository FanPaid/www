Creator Guidelines

Guidelines for creators publishing content, building communities, livestreaming, selling products, and monetizing through FanPaid.

Last updated: July 13, 2026

1. Be Authentic and Accurate

Represent yourself, qualifications, affiliations, products, services, and offers accurately. Do not impersonate others or use deceptive claims to obtain money or engagement.

2. Publish Content You Have Rights To

Only publish content you created or are authorized to use. Obtain necessary licenses, permissions, releases, and consents.

3. Clearly Describe Paid Offers

Clearly describe subscriptions, paid content, digital products, bookings, campaigns, and deliverables. Disclose material limitations and recurring charges where applicable.

4. Build Communities Responsibly

Moderate responsibly and do not encourage harassment, fraud, coordinated abuse, or evasion of FanPaid safety systems.

5. Livestream Responsibly

Creators are responsible for content appearing on their streams. Illegal activity, dangerous exploitation, prohibited content, and non-consenting persons in private contexts are not allowed.

6. Age-Restricted Categories

If FanPaid permits an age-restricted category, creators may be required to complete government ID, liveness, age, consent, co-performer documentation, re-verification, and enhanced moderation or recordkeeping workflows. Eligibility is not automatic.

7. Payments and Payouts

Do not manipulate tips, subscriptions, referrals, refunds, disputes, or payouts. Creators must provide accurate payout and compliance information.

8. AI and Synthetic Media

AI may not be used to impersonate real people, create non-consensual intimate imagery, evade identity controls, or materially mislead users. Disclosures may be required for certain synthetic media.

9. Review and Enforcement

FanPaid may use automated signals, human review, user reports, and specialist escalation. Additional information or verification may be requested.

10. Questions

Contact creators@fanpaid.com or hello@fanpaid.com.
