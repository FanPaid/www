Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FanPaid collects, uses, shares, retains, and protects personal information.

FanPaid is currently in pre-launch development. This Policy describes the privacy practices for the FanPaid website and, where applicable, the FanPaid platform and services as they become available.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how FanPaid ("FanPaid," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you visit fanpaid.com, communicate with us, request early access, or use FanPaid products and services that link to this Policy.

This Policy does not apply to third-party websites, applications, or services that maintain their own privacy policies.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

Depending on how you interact with FanPaid, you may provide information such as your name, email address, username, business or organization information, profile details, communications, support requests, content, and other information you choose to submit.

When platform features become available, we may also collect account information, creator or business profile information, payment and payout-related details, tax or compliance information, identity verification information, and records related to transactions or platform activity.

Information collected automatically

We may collect device and usage information such as IP address, browser type, operating system, referring pages, pages viewed, approximate location derived from IP address, timestamps, diagnostic data, and interactions with our website or services.

Information from third parties

We may receive information from service providers and integration partners, including identity verification providers, payment processors, hosting providers, analytics services, communications providers, anti-fraud services, and other services you choose to connect to FanPaid.

3. How We Use Information

We may use personal information to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve FanPaid.
  • Create and administer accounts, profiles, communities, and organizations.
  • Process requests, transactions, subscriptions, payouts, and related records.
  • Authenticate users and perform identity, trust, safety, fraud, and security checks.
  • Enable communications, support, notifications, and customer service.
  • Personalize experiences, recommendations, search, discovery, and platform features.
  • Measure performance, understand usage, troubleshoot issues, and develop new features.
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and platform policies.
  • Comply with legal, tax, accounting, regulatory, and law-enforcement obligations.
  • Send product, service, and promotional communications where permitted by law. You may opt out of marketing emails at any time.

4. How We Share Information

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers. With vendors that help us operate infrastructure, storage, communications, analytics, security, verification, payments, support, and other business functions.
  • Other users. Information you intentionally make public or share through profiles, posts, communities, creator pages, businesses, organizations, or other collaborative features may be visible to other users.
  • Business transactions. In connection with a merger, financing, acquisition, restructuring, bankruptcy, or sale of all or part of our business or assets.
  • Legal and safety reasons. When we believe disclosure is reasonably necessary to comply with law, respond to lawful process, protect rights or safety, investigate abuse or fraud, or enforce our agreements.
  • With your direction or consent. When you ask us to share information or authorize an integration or other disclosure.

We do not sell personal information for money. If our data practices change in a way that creates additional privacy rights under applicable law, we will provide the notices and choices required by law.

5. Payments and Financial Information

Payments, payouts, or related financial services may be provided by third-party payment or financial service providers. Those providers may collect and process payment credentials, wallet information, identity details, transaction information, and other information required to provide their services and satisfy legal or compliance obligations.

FanPaid may receive transaction identifiers, payment status, limited account or wallet details, billing information, and other records necessary to administer transactions, prevent fraud, provide support, and maintain accounting records. Third-party providers process information under their own terms and privacy policies.

6. Cookies, Local Storage, and Analytics

We may use cookies, local storage, pixels, software development kits, logs, and similar technologies to operate the service, remember preferences, maintain security, understand usage, and improve performance.

Where required by applicable law, we will provide appropriate notice or consent controls for non-essential tracking technologies. Browser settings may also allow you to restrict certain cookies, although some service functionality may be affected.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including to provide services, maintain business and transaction records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, satisfy legal obligations, protect the platform, and prevent fraud or abuse.

Retention periods may vary based on the type of information, the nature of our relationship with you, legal requirements, security needs, and whether information is needed to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims.

8. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your credentials and for promptly notifying us if you believe your account or information has been compromised.

9. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live and subject to applicable law, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or a copy of certain personal information; to object to or restrict certain processing; or to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

You may also have rights concerning certain disclosures, targeted advertising, or the use of sensitive personal information under applicable law. We may need to verify your identity before completing a request and may retain information where permitted or required by law.

To submit a privacy request, email privacy@fanpaid.com. If that address is not yet provisioned, you may contact hello@fanpaid.com.

You may opt out of marketing communications by using the unsubscribe mechanism in an applicable message or by contacting us. Service, security, transactional, or legal notices may still be sent when necessary.

10. International Users and Data Transfers

FanPaid is intended to serve users in multiple countries. Information may be processed or stored in countries other than the country where you live. Those countries may have different data protection laws.

Where required, we will use appropriate mechanisms for international transfers of personal information and take steps designed to protect information in accordance with applicable law.

11. Children's Privacy

FanPaid is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Certain platform features, creator tools, payment features, or content categories may have higher minimum age requirements.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to us in violation of applicable requirements, contact us so we can investigate and take appropriate action.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as FanPaid develops, our practices change, or legal requirements evolve. We will post the updated Policy on this page and revise the "Last updated" date. Where required by law, we will provide additional notice.
