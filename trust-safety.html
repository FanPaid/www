Trust & Safety

FanPaid is designing identity, moderation, fraud prevention, consent, and platform safety systems as core product infrastructure.

FanPaid is in active pre-launch development. This page describes our safety direction and planned control framework; specific controls will be documented as features become available.

Our Approach

Trust and safety is not intended to be added after launch. FanPaid is designing safety controls alongside accounts, monetization, communities, livestreaming, and payments.

Identity and Authenticity

Depending on account type and feature eligibility, FanPaid may require government ID, selfie or liveness verification, age checks, business verification, or additional review.

Content Moderation

Our planned moderation architecture combines reporting, policy rules, automated risk signals, human review, escalation workflows, and audit logs.

Protecting Minors

We prohibit child sexual exploitation and are designing age gates, verification requirements, reporting workflows, restricted-feature controls, and evidence-preservation processes.

Consent and Depicted Persons

Creators must obtain necessary consent and rights for people appearing in content. Certain categories may require identity and consent records for depicted persons or collaborators.

Fraud and Platform Abuse

FanPaid plans to use account, device, transaction, behavioral, and verification signals to identify fraud, duplicate abuse, account compromise, payment manipulation, and enforcement evasion.

Privacy and Access Controls

We are designing role-based permissions, audit records, secure credential handling, and privacy-aware access systems. See our Privacy Policy.

Report a Safety Concern

Contact safety@fanpaid.com. For urgent danger, contact local emergency services first.
